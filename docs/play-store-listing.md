## Full description (4000 chars max)
Torevo is the all-in-one platform for hosting and playing local sports events.
Whether you're an organizer running a weekend tournament or a player looking for
your next match, Torevo brings the whole experience into one app.

PLAY
• Discover sports events near you — cricket, football, kabaddi, basketball, pickleball, chess and more.
• Filter by sport, area, date and time, and sort to find the right event fast.
• Register as an individual or as a full team — the captain adds every teammate by mobile number.
• Edit your team's lineup any time until the tournament ends.
• Secure entry fees: your payment is held in escrow and released to the organizer only as the event proceeds, with refunds handled automatically when applicable.
• Follow your favourite sports and hosts and get notified the moment a new event drops.

WATCH LIVE
• Follow matches ball-by-ball and goal-by-goal with live scoreboards.
• Full statistics for every game and every player — batting and bowling figures, team scores, and career records, the way real sports stats should look.

HOST
• Create events tailored to your sport: set the number of teams, players per team, and choose per-team or per-person entry fees.
• Manage registrations and your team roster from a single dashboard.
• Score matches yourself or assign an umpire — only the host or assigned umpire can enter scores, so results stay accurate.
• Keep records across every event you run.

Switch between Play and Host modes anytime with a single tap.

Torevo — host it, play it, score it.
