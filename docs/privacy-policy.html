Privacy Policy for Torevo

Last updated: 21 June 2026

Torevo ("we", "our", or "the app") is a sports event hosting and participation platform. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices. By using Torevo you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: your name and the mobile phone number you provide when registering or creating a team.
  • Team & registration data: the names and mobile numbers of teammates that a team captain enters in order to register for an event.
  • Event & gameplay data: events you host or join, registrations, and match/score statistics linked to your profile or phone number.
  • Payment & escrow data: when you register for paid events, transaction and escrow records are processed and stored to manage entry fees, holds, and refunds. Card/UPI details are handled by our payment processor and are not stored by Torevo.
  • Device & usage data: basic technical information needed for the app to function (e.g. network state).

2. How We Use Information

  • To create and manage your account and team registrations.
  • To run events, process entry fees through escrow, and issue refunds.
  • To record and display match scores and player/team statistics.
  • To send you relevant notifications about events you host, join, or follow (when enabled).
  • To maintain the security and integrity of the platform.

3. How Information Is Stored

App data is stored securely using our backend provider (Supabase). Access is protected by row-level security rules so that users can only access data they are permitted to see.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. Information is shared only:

  • With event hosts/organizers as needed to manage registrations and scoring for events you join.
  • With service providers (backend hosting, payment processing) that help us operate the app.
  • Where required by law.

5. Data Retention

We retain account, event, and statistics data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service and maintain records. You may request deletion of your account and associated personal data (see Contact below).

6. Your Choices

  • You can edit team member details until an event/tournament ends.
  • You can turn notifications on or off in the app.
  • You can request access to or deletion of your personal data by contacting us.

7. Children's Privacy

Torevo is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
